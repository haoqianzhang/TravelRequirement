Additional Information:

  allowed to enter Najaf (NJF). 
- Letters signed and stamped issued by the Ministry of
  Interior of Iraq to passengers arriving at Baghdad (BGW),
  Basra (BSR) or Najaf (NJF) must be forwarded to the
  airline's station manager in order to receive an OK TO BOARD

  from the immigration. 

- Letters signed and stamped issued by the Ministry of
  Interior of the Kurdistan Regional Government to passengers
  arriving at Erbil (EBL) and Sulaymaniyah (ISU) must be
  forwarded to the airline's station manager in order to

  receive an OK TO BOARD from the immigration.

Warning:
- Passengers are subject to medical screening and quarantine.

- Passengers traveling to Erbil (EBL) who have been since the
  15 of December 2020 in Australia, Austria, Belgium, Brazil,

 - This does not apply to foreign nationals of Iraqi origin.

 - This does not apply to passengers younger than 10 years.
   They will be subject to a PCR test on arrival.  
- Passengers traveling to Basra (BSR) must have a printed

 - This does not apply to passengers younger than 10 years.

  before departure from the first embarkation point.

 - This does not apply to passengers younger than 10 years.

 - This does not apply to passengers younger than 11 years.
