Additional Information:

- Passengers with a resident visa traveling to Kuwait for the
  first time must have:

  - the resident visa authenticated by Kuwait Embassy in the

  country of residence, and
  - an original Criminal Clearance Certificate authenticated

  by Kuwait Embassy in the country of residence. 
  In case of no Kuwait Embassy in the country of residence,
  authentication is not required. 

- Passengers with a work permit traveling to Kuwait for the
  first time are required to hold an original Criminal

  Clearance Certificate authenticated by Kuwait Embassy in the

  country of residence. In case of no Kuwait Embassy in the
  country of residence, authentication is not required.

Warning:

  Syria, United Kingdom and Yemen. 
- Passengers must have a medical certificate with a negative

  Coronavirus (COVID-19) PCR test result. The test must have
  been taken at most 72 hours before departure.
 - This does not apply to passengers younger than 6 years.
 - This does not apply to passengers returning to Kuwait with
   a medical certificate with a negative Coronavirus

   (COVID-19) PCR test result issued before departure from
   Kuwait. They must have been a maximum of 4 days abroad.

  self-isolation for 14 days.

  considered valid until the day before the expiration date.
