Passport required.
- Passports and other documents accepted for entry must be

Visa required, except for Nationals of China (People's Rep.)

Visa Issuance:
Visa required, except for Nationals of China (People's Rep.)

can obtain a visa on arrival for a maximum stay of 7 days.
They must have return/onward tickets. They can apply to extend

their stay for an additional 90 days. 

Visa required, except for Passengers with a Hong Kong (SAR
China) passport can obtain a visa on arrival for a maximum
stay of 7 days. They must have return/onward tickets. They can

apply to extend their stay for an additional 90 days.

Visa required, except for Passengers with a Macao (SAR China)
passport can obtain a visa on arrival for a maximum stay of 7
days. They must have return/onward tickets. They can apply to

extend their stay for an additional 90 days.

Minors:
- Children under 15 years of age, traveling alone or
  accompanied by adults, must hold either a passport or a
  "Carnet de Voyage". 
Warning:
- A "Traveler Form" must be completed online at
  https://voyage.gouv.tg . 
- Passengers must have a medical certificate with a negative

  Coronavirus (COVID-19) PCR test result. The test must have
  been taken at most 7 days before arrival. The test must be
  uploaded at https://voyage.gouv.tg/checkphone . 
- Passengers are subject to a Coronavirus (COVID-19) PCR test
  upon arrival. Passengers must pay for the test before
  departure at https://voyage.gouv.tg/pay/fees. This will
  generate a QR code which must be presented upon arrival.
