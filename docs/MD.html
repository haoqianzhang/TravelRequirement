 Moldova (Rep.) (MD)



Passport required.
- Passports and other documents accepted for entry must be
  valid for a minimum of 3 months beyond the period of
  intended stay.
Admission and Transit Restrictions:

- Passengers arriving from Switzerland are not allowed to
  enter. 
 - This does not apply to family members of nationals of

   Moldova (Rep.).
 - This does not apply to family members of passengers with a
   residence permit issued by Moldova (Rep.). 
 - This does not apply to students.

Visa required, except for Passengers with a Hong Kong (SAR
China) passport for a maximum stay of 90 days. 
Visa required, except for Passengers with a Macao (SAR China)
passport for a maximum stay of 90 days. 
Visa required, except for Nationals of China (People's Rep.)

with a normal passport and a residence permit issued by
Switzerland for a maximum stay of 90 days. The residence
permit must be valid for the period of intended stay.
Visa Issuance:
- E-visas can be obtained before departure at
  www.evisa.gov.md. Passengers must have a printed e-visa
  confirmation. 

Minors:
- Passengers younger than 15 years can enter with any identity

  document with a photo. 
- Passengers younger than 18 years must travel accompanied by
  one of their parents or an adult other than a parent. When
  accompanied by an adult other than a parent, minors must
  have a declaration with a notarized signature of the parent.

  The declaration must contain details of the accompanying

  adult, purpose of the journey, its duration and the country

  of destination. 

Additional Information:

- A maximum stay of 90 days is granted within 6 months to
  passengers with a Hong Kong (SAR China) passport. 
- A maximum stay of 90 days is granted within 6 months to
  passengers with a Macao (SAR China) passport.
- A maximum stay of 90 days is granted within 180 days to
  nationals of China (People's Rep.) with a residence permit

  issued by Switzerland. 
Warning:
- Passengers could be subject to self-isolation for 14 days. 

- Visitors not holding return/onward tickets could be refused

  entry.  (SEE NOTE 43931) 
    NOTE 43931: Exempt are minors under 16 years of age.
