Minors:
- Minors traveling unaccompanied must hold a written approval
  from the parents and a contact address in Rwanda.
Warning:
- Passengers must complete a Health Declaration Form before

  departure at https://travel.rbc.gov.rw/ . 
- Passengers must have a medical certificate with a negative

  Coronavirus (COVID-19) PCR test result. The test must have
  been taken at most 120 hours before departure from the first
  embarkation point.

- Passengers are subject to Coronavirus (COVID-19) test upon
  arrival and quarantine for 24 hours.
